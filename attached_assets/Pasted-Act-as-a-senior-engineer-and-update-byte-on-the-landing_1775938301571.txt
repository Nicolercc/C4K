Hero Section Background (the big top area)
Two layers stacked on top of each other:

Bottom layer — AI-generated image via Stable Diffusion (the readdy.ai/api/search-image endpoint):
Prompt: “colorful abstract geometric shapes floating stars sparkles teal purple yellow gradient background playful kids educational technology vibrant cheerful illustration with soft bokeh light effects”
It’s a landscape image (1440×800) set to bg-cover bg-center — so it fills the full hero width
Think: floaty geometric shapes, sparkles, soft glowing bokeh blobs in teal/purple/yellow
Top layer — dark gradient overlay sitting on top of that image:
bg-gradient-to-br from-violet-900/70 via-purple-800/60 to-teal-900/70
This is a diagonal dark purple-to-teal overlay at 60-70% opacity — it darkens the image so the white text stays readable
So the hero looks like: glowing abstract art underneath, moody dark purple-teal tint on top, white text floating over it.

Rest of the Page Background
The overall page uses:

bg-gradient-to-br from-violet-50 via-white to-teal-50
A super soft, barely-there diagonal gradient — light lavender → white → light mint. Very subtle, almost white.

Section-specific backgrounds:

Features section — plain white/transparent, cards have their own colored backgrounds (teal-50, yellow-50, pink-50, etc.)
Lesson Path section — bg-gradient-to-br from-purple-50 to-teal-50 (soft pastel purple-to-mint)

CTA section — bg-gradient-to-r from-teal-500 to-teal-600 (solid teal banner)
